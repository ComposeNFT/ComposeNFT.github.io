Roadmap
ComposeNFT Roadmap for 2022 and 2023

October 2022

ComposeNFT Launch

Artists will be able to create Master Copy NFTs and license their music out to content creators, verifiable on the blockchain

November 2022

Layer 2 Support

ComposeNFT will be the first music platform built on Layer 2 Ethereum powered by zkSync. Layer 2 allows us to guarantee low gas prices at scale. ComposeNFT will be built on top of zkSync 2.0, a EVM-compatible ZK Rollup powered by zkEVM.

February 2023

ComposeNFT v2

ComposeNFT v2 will feature streaming royalty support, where NFT owners can earn a percentage of artists' streaming revenues.

2023

ComposeNFT v3

3rd Party license verification API, NFT Album Support, new licenses and more coming
